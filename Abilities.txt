Dive Attack - If the enemy is flying and dives 30 ft straight towards a target and then hits it with a melee attack, the attack deals an extra 12(3d6) damage.
Ionic Shield - The enemy has an ionic shield with 15 hp, where the shield always takes damage until it gets to 0, upon which the enemy takes damage.
Amphibious - The enemy can breathe air and water.
Probing Telepathy - If you communicate with the enemy telepathically, the enemy can now see your deepest desire (if you are visible).
Fear of Fire - If the enemy takes fire damage, it has disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks until the end of its next turn.
Infrared - The enemy has advantage on Perception (Wisdom) checks that rely on sight or heat.
Snow Camouflage - The enemy has advantage on Stealth (Dexterity) checks made in snowy terrain.
Desert Camouflage - The enemy has advantage on Stealth (Dexterity) checks made in desert terrain.
Jungle Camouflage - The enemy has advantage on Stealth (Dexterity) checks made in jungle terrain.
Urban Camouflage - The enemy has advantage on Stealth (Dexterity) checks made in urban terrain.
Pack Tactics - The enemy has advantage on an attack roll against a target if at least one of its allies is within 5 ft of the target and the ally isn't incapacitated.
Dwarven Resilience - The enemy has advantage on saving throws against poison.
Strong Lungs - The enemy can hold their breath underwater or in space for an extra 18 seconds (3 rounds) before succumbing to the effects.
Prone - If the enemy goes prone and holds their action for 1 turn, they have advantage on their next ranged attack roll.
Slide - If the enemy slides into an enemy (and remains prone afterwards), they have advantage on that melee attack roll.
Multiattack - The enemy can make two attacks.
